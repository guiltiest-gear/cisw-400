Reverse PIG

There are two players, and both start with 30 points. The player who's turn it is makes a wager of points, ranging from 2 to 12, and rolls the dice.

Depending on the value of the dice and your wager, the following will occur according to these rules:

  • If your wager is less than the roll, you lose the wagered points.
  • If your wager is greater than the roll, your opponent loses the wagered points.
  • If your wager is exactly the value of the roll, you gain the wagered points.

Note: To keep a fair balance, if you roll exactly, your turn must be passed to the next player.

There is a special case with snake eyes (being two ones), as your points will be reset to 30.

The first person to 0 points or lower loses.
